Parameters

\( \begin{cases} 1h & \mbox{if $x \leq s$} \cr h(1 - 2\left((x - s) / (e-s)\right)^2) & \mbox{if $x \leq 0.5(s+e)$}\cr h(2 \left((x - e) / (e-s)\right)^2) & \mbox{if $x < e$}\cr 0h & \mbox{otherwise} \end{cases}\)

where \(h\) is the height of the Term, \(s\) is the start of the ZShape, \(e\) is the end of the ZShape.
